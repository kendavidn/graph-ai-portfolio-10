About Me

I am a scientist, writer, and educator with a background in biology, public health, and data science. Currently pursuing an MSc in Global Public Health at the University of Geneva, I explore the intersection of generative AI and research workflows. As founder of ScienceMeSoftly.com, I aim to make science communication accessible. My experiences range from teaching biology and physics to leading international expeditions and conducting research at MIT. I am passionate about using AI tools to accelerate scientific discovery, education, and communication.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course organized by GRAPH Courses (University of Geneva). It focuses on no-code AI tools for automating research and professional tasks. The program combines academic rigor with hands-on practice to equip learners with practical skills in AI-powered workflows.

Key Features

  • Weekly live workshops and help sessions
  • Private forums and personalized feedback
  • Capstone project with coaching and showcase
  • Shareable certificate & lifetime resource access

Course Modules

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations with AI
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis
  • Generative AI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI for Task Automation
  • Generative AI for Communication & Outreach
  • Capstone Project & Showcase
